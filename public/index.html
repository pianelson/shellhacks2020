Chat with Luna

Having a hard time processing what you're feeling? Luna is here to help.

Launch Chat

an online virtual companion built with AI and love

Luna is designed to serve as a mental check-in for Twitter users whose recent tweets have been deemed to be negative.

As an online chat assistant, she helps users find resources to address their emotions in an attempt to reduce negative patterns in social media activity and bring more positivity to the online sphere.

Built with love for Shellhacks 2020.
